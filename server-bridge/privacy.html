Privacy Policy

Last updated: · Draft — have this reviewed by an attorney before publishing.

This Privacy Policy describes what information Lee Crowe Software Solutions LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects in connection with ServerBridge and this website, and how we use it.

What we don't collect

ServerBridge does not upload, store, or have access to the files you migrate. Migration traffic flows directly between your file server and your own Microsoft 365 tenant via the Microsoft Graph API, using your own sign-in credentials. We never see your file contents, your Microsoft 365 tenant data, or your authentication tokens.

What we do collect

  • License and billing information. When you purchase ServerBridge Pro, our payment processor, Stripe, collects your payment details directly — we never see or store your card number. We receive your email address to issue your license key.
  • License key activation. The ServerBridge desktop app sends your license key and a randomly generated device identifier (not tied to any personal hardware identifier) to our licensing server to activate and periodically re-validate your license.
  • Website contact requests. If you email us (e.g. to ask a question or request a refund), we keep that correspondence to respond to you.

How we use this information

Solely to provide and bill for ServerBridge Pro, respond to support requests, and meet our legal and tax obligations. We do not sell your information or use it for advertising.

Third parties

Payment data is processed by Stripe; license records are stored in Microsoft Azure (Azure Table Storage). Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing the data they process.

Data retention

We retain license and billing records for as long as needed to provide the service and to meet tax and accounting obligations. You can request deletion of your account data by emailing us, subject to records we're legally required to keep.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email hello@leecrowesoftware.com.
